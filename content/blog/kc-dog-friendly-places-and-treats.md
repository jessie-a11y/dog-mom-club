Kansas City is one of the best cities to be a dog mom. Between the parks, the patios, the farmers markets, and the community events, there's always somewhere new to explore with your pup by your side. Here's our guide to enjoying KC with your dog — and the treats that make every outing a little sweeter.

## Start the Day at a Dog Friendly Park

Kansas City has some incredible green spaces where dogs can run, explore, and burn off energy. Off leash dog parks are a favorite for social pups while walking trails are perfect for relaxed mornings together. Bringing a few treats along makes it easy to reward good behavior or practice training while you're out. Winston's Woofles and Beagle's Bagels are great options to toss in your bag for a quick snack during playtime.

## Grab Lunch at a Dog Friendly Patio

After a morning outside, KC dog moms love stopping at patios where pups can relax at your feet while you enjoy lunch. Many local restaurants and breweries welcome leashed dogs outdoors, making it easy to include them in your plans. Having your own dog treats on hand means your pup doesn't feel left out while you eat and keeps them happy and settled while you enjoy the moment.

## Explore Local Dog Friendly Events

Kansas City hosts plenty of pet friendly events throughout the year including outdoor markets, seasonal festivals, and dog focused celebrations. These are perfect chances to socialize your dog, meet other dog moms, and enjoy the community. A small bag of treats makes these outings smoother and helps reward calm behavior in busy environments.

## More Ways to Enjoy KC With Your Dog

Beyond parks and patios, Kansas City offers walking paths, scenic neighborhoods, and outdoor spaces that are perfect for leisurely strolls. Whether you're exploring downtown areas, visiting outdoor art spaces, or enjoying a quiet evening walk, having treats on hand adds a little joy to every adventure.
