When you love your dog like family, the treats you choose become more than rewards — they're part of your pup's everyday nutrition. At Dog Mom Club, we bake every batch in Overland Park, KS and Riverside, MO with one mission: make treats that are just as wholesome as the food we put on our own plates.

Clean ingredients aren't a trend. They're a standard. And here's why they matter so much for your dog.

## Dogs Deserve Real Ingredients — Not Fillers

Walk down any pet aisle and you'll see long, complicated labels full of vague terms like:

- "dried bakery product"
- "animal digest"
- "natural flavor"
- "meat by-product"

These ingredients hide behind generic names and exist for one reason: to lower the cost of manufacturing.

But cheap fillers create digestive issues, allergic reactions, and often provide little to no nutritional value.

Your dog deserves better.

That's why Dog Mom Club treats use only recognizable, whole ingredients such as:

- Oat flour, almond flour, and whole wheat flour
- Natural peanut butter
- Unsweetened applesauce
- Cheese blends and powders
- Coconut oil
- Beet root, blue spirulina, and other natural food colorings

No fillers. No shortcuts. No ingredients we wouldn't give our own pups — Doug, Winston, Lucy, and Layla.

## Clean Ingredients Make Treats More Digestible

Dogs thrive on consistency and simplicity. When treats contain 10–20+ mystery ingredients, their bodies have to work much harder to process them.

Clean treats:

- Reduce bloating
- Support healthier digestion
- Are safer for sensitive stomachs
- Keep your dog's gut healthy

This is especially important for pups with allergies or food sensitivities. Our three core treat flavors — Beagle's Bagels, Winston's Woofles, and Lucy's Loopies — are made with minimal ingredients so you always know exactly what you're feeding.

## Superfood Colors Are Safer Than Artificial Dyes

Bright, colorful treats don't have to come from synthetic dyes.

Our pinks, blues, and golden yellows come from:

- **Beet root** (pink/red)
- **Blue spirulina** (blue/green)
- **Cheddar powder** (yellow/orange)

These superfoods don't just add color — they add nutrition.

Artificial dyes have been linked to hyperactivity, stomach irritation, and long-term inflammation. That's why we never use them. Ever.

## No Dried Bakery Product — Here's Why That Matters

"Dried bakery product" is one of the most common fillers in commercial dog treats — and one of the least understood.

It often includes stale leftover bread, discarded crackers, cookie scraps, baked goods with unknown preservatives, and added sugar, salt, and other additives.

Manufacturers use it because it's cheap.

We refuse to use it because your dog's health is priceless.

Every Dog Mom Club treat starts with oat flour, almond flour, or whole wheat flour for a clean, gentle, nutrient-rich base.

Small-batch baking means better texture, richer flavor, fewer preservatives required, and maximum freshness.

## Clean Ingredients Support Overall Wellness

When treats are clean and simple, you're supporting:

- Clearer skin
- Shinier coats
- Stable energy levels
- Stronger immune health
- Fewer digestive problems
- Healthier weight maintenance

Treats should add to your dog's health — never take away from it.
