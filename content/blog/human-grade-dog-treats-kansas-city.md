When it comes to our dogs, most of us don't think of them as pets. They're family. They sleep in our beds, ride shotgun, and show up for us on our hardest days. So when it comes to what they eat, it makes sense to care about the ingredients going into their treats.

At Dog Mom Club, we choose to use human grade ingredients because we believe dogs deserve the same quality and care we expect for ourselves. Here's what that really means and why it matters for your pup.

## What Does Human Grade Really Mean

Human grade ingredients are made to meet standards for human consumption. This means the ingredients are sourced, handled, and prepared in a way that meets food safety requirements for people, not just animals.

Many mass produced dog treats use ingredients that are considered feed grade. These ingredients are legally allowed in pet food but are not required to meet the same quality or sourcing standards as food made for humans.

Using human grade ingredients gives us more confidence in what goes into every batch of treats we make.

## Fewer Fillers and Ingredients You Can Recognize

One of the biggest benefits of human grade ingredients is simplicity. When ingredients are high quality, there is less need for fillers, preservatives, or artificial flavors.

If you read the ingredient list on Dog Mom Club treats, you will recognize what you see. No mystery powders. No long chemical names. Just simple ingredients that serve a purpose.

That makes treats easier on sensitive stomachs and gives dog moms peace of mind.

## Better for Dogs With Sensitive Stomachs

Many dogs experience upset stomachs, gas, or inconsistent digestion. While every dog is different, lower quality treats can often make those issues worse.

Human grade ingredients tend to be gentler and easier to digest. That is especially helpful for dogs who are picky eaters or who have had reactions to heavily processed treats in the past.

We hear often from customers who say their dogs tolerate our treats better, even when they struggle with others.

## Small Batch Means More Care in Every Treat

Using human grade ingredients goes hand in hand with how we make our treats. Dog Mom Club treats are handmade in small batches, not mass produced in factories.

That allows us to pay attention to texture, freshness, and consistency. It also means we can adjust recipes thoughtfully instead of rushing production.

Every batch is made with intention, not speed.

## Why This Matters to Kansas City Dog Moms

As a local Kansas City business, we know our customers care about where their products come from. Many KC dog moms shop local, support small businesses, and want transparency in the brands they choose.

Using human grade ingredients aligns with those values. It reflects the same care you put into your own home, your food, and your family.

When you give your dog a Dog Mom Club treat, you are choosing something made with real ingredients, by real people, right here in the Kansas City area.

We use human grade ingredients because your dog deserves more than the bare minimum. They deserve treats made with care, honesty, and love.
